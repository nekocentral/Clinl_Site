Mailing lists:

announce@clinl.org:
Join this by sending an empty email to announce+subscribe@clinl.org, you will get an email back with instructions. Only moderators can post to this list.

To unsubscribe - email announce+unsubscribe@clinl.org
To get a list of commands - announce+help@clinl.org

list@clinl.org:
Join this by sending an empty email to list+subscribe@clinl.org, you will get an email back with instructions.
Every subscriber can post to this list. Please rememember to press "Reply List" when answering an email from the list in order to avoid that you accidently send the email directly to the person you indend to answer.

To unsubscribe - list+unsubscribe@clinl.org
To get a list of commands - list+help@clinl.org
